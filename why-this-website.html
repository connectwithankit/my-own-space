WHY THIS WEBSITE

I found a pressing need to make my own site and post my own content.

I dabbled with a twitter account and wordpress blog but was so tired

of all the unnecessary features I didn't need to put my word out.

That is when I came across Derek Sivers

and instantly fell in love with the simple design using HTML and CSS and realised how

it should have been done all along. I emailed Derek with a specific ask and in reply

got the template for this website.

Afterall, all we have done is by standing on the shoulder of giants

I plan to use this as my personal workspace. No fancy substacks

no over the top designs, just me and my simple html and css.
